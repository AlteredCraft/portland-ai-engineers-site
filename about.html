Our Mission

Portland AI Engineers is a community of software developers passionate about building practical applications using generative AI technologies. Our core focus is on solving real-world problems by leveraging language models and AI tools.

What is an AI Engineer?

"A software developer who leverages existing language models. Using them to both help build applications and add capabilities into products that solve real-world problems."

Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive environment where everyone can learn and grow together.

Our Commitment

Portland AI Engineers is a community-first organization that values in-person connection, continuous learning, and inclusive participation. We welcome members of all experience levels and backgrounds. Everyone has valuable perspectives to share, and we grow stronger by learning from each other. This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including meetups, online discussions, and any interactions representing our community.

Expected Behaviors

  • Be respectful and inclusive

    Treat all participants with dignity regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

  • Use welcoming language

    Communicate professionally and considerately. Be mindful of how your words may be received.

  • Accept feedback gracefully

    Constructive criticism helps us all improve. Receive it openly and offer it kindly.

  • Assume good intent

    Interpret others' statements in good faith. Focus on learning together rather than winning arguments.

  • Protect privacy

    Respect confidentiality. Don't share personal information or photos without consent.

Zero Tolerance

The following behaviors will result in immediate removal:

  • Harassment of any kind, including unwelcome attention, intimidation, or stalking
  • Personal attacks, trolling, insults, or derogatory comments
  • Discriminatory behavior of any kind
  • Sexualized language or imagery in any community space
  • Sharing private information without consent
  • Disruptive behavior that interferes with events or others' participation
Enforcement

Organizers will take appropriate action in response to any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, including warning, temporary ban, or permanent removal from the community.

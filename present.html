Presentation Guidelines

Tips for creating engaging presentations

Do's
  • Focus on practical applications
  • Include interactive elements
  • Use visual aids and demos
  • Prepare for Q&A session
  • Test technical setup in advance
  • Provide additional resources
Don'ts
  • Use as commercial advertisement
  • Exceed time limits
  • Assume technical expertise
  • Ignore audience questions
  • Skip preparation
  • Forget to test equipment

How to Submit Your Presentation

Include the following information:

Presentation Tips

Make your presentation memorable

Keep it Focused

Choose a clear, specific topic rather than trying to cover too much ground.

Tell a Story

Structure your presentation with a beginning, middle, and end.

Practice Timing

Rehearse to ensure you stay within your allotted time.

Engage Your Audience

Ask questions, run polls, or include interactive demos.

Prepare for Questions

Anticipate what the audience might ask and have answers ready.

Share Resources

Provide links, code repos, or slides for deeper exploration.

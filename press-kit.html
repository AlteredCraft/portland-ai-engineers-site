Usage Guidelines

How to use our brand assets correctly

Do's
  • Use the full-color logo on white or light backgrounds
  • Maintain the logo's original proportions
  • Use the monochrome variant when color isn't available
  • Provide adequate clear space around the logo
  • Use provided color values for brand consistency
Don'ts
  • Alter the logo colors or apply filters
  • Stretch, distort, or rotate the logo
  • Place the color logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Add effects such as shadows, outlines, or gradients
  • Recreate or approximate the logo with different fonts
